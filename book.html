What is the will of God?

This Christian journey, called life, is not meant to be traveled alone. Join me and we can walk through these biblical principles together. At some point, on the journey, we all want to know God's will for our lives.

As I did, we want to walk in our purpose and grow towards our divine destiny. It is very challenging for many to clearly discover their purpose. This personal study guide will give you specific biblical principles that are God's will for your life. No matter your spiritual maturity, start today, start here, start now, and allow this personal study guide to help you to level up.

Following these biblical principles can give you confidence that your life will be aligned with God's will. After consistently following the action items as outlined in this personal study guide, you can feel confident that you now know the will of God for your life and you are walking, talking, thinking, and living in his will. Today, take this first step and seek to know the will of God for your life.
